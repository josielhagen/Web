Wedding Planning Packages

Choose the level of support that fits your season. All packages include a complimentary consultation and customized planning checklist.

6-Month Agreement Plan

Full-service guidance for the final stretch.

  • Planning support beginning 6 months before your wedding date
  • Monthly planning meetings (virtual or in-person)
  • Vendor recommendations and communication support
  • Detailed wedding-day timeline + master schedule
  • Rehearsal coordination
  • Day-of coordination (8–10 hours on-site)

Perfect for couples who want a partner in the final half year of planning.

3-Month Plan

Coordination plus light planning support.

  • Planning support beginning 3 months before your wedding
  • Bi-weekly check-ins leading up to your date
  • Vendor confirmation and final detail review
  • Custom wedding-day timeline
  • Rehearsal run-through and wedding party briefing
  • Day-of coordination (8 hours on-site)

Ideal if most decisions are made and you need help pulling it all together.

Day-Of Coordination

You plan it—Josie runs it.

  • Initial consultation + planning questionnaire
  • Detail meeting 4–6 weeks before the wedding
  • Creation of wedding-day timeline
  • Vendor confirmation the week of the wedding
  • Day-of coordination (6–8 hours on-site)
  • Point person for vendors & family

Best for DIY planners who want to relax and enjoy the day.

The Joyful Planning Process

  3. Plan: We build timelines, confirm vendors, and walk through details.
  4. Celebrate: On your wedding day, you focus on marrying your person—I’ll handle the rest.
